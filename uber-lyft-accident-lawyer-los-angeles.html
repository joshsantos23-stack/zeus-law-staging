Uber and Lyft Accident Claims Are Complex

Rideshare accidents involve multiple layers of insurance: the driver personal policy, the Uber or Lyft commercial policy (up to $1M per incident), and potentially underinsured motorist coverage. The coverage that applies depends on whether the driver was active on the app, en route to pick up a rider, or carrying a passenger at the time of the accident.
