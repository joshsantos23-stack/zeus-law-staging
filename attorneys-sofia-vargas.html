About Sofia Vargas

Sofia Vargas joined ZEUS Law Firm after earning recognition as one of Los Angeles' most effective personal injury litigators. Named to the Top 40 Under 40 list by the National Trial Lawyers, she brings courtroom intensity that insurance carriers have learned to take seriously.

Her clients describe her as relentless, responsive, and strategic. She returns every call personally. That's the ZEUS standard.
