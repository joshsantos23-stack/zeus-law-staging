FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

Answers From
Our Attorneys.

The questions we hear most often — answered plainly, without the legal fog.

How much does a personal injury lawyer cost?+

At ZEUS, we work on a contingency fee basis — you pay nothing unless we win. No upfront costs, no retainer, no hourly fees.

How long do I have to file a personal injury claim in California?+

California's statute of limitations is 2 years from the accident date. Government entity cases may have a 6-month deadline. Contact us immediately to protect your rights.

What is my personal injury case worth?+

Case value depends on medical expenses, lost wages, future care costs, and pain and suffering. We've recovered settlements from $100K to over $5 million. Contact us for a free case evaluation.

How long will my case take?+

Most cases settle within 6 to 18 months. Complex cases involving severe injuries or disputed liability may take longer.

What should I do immediately after an accident?+

1. Call 911. 2. Get medical attention. 3. Document everything. 4. Don't talk to the other party's insurance. 5. Call ZEUS Law Firm immediately — we're available 24/7.

Do I need a lawyer if the insurance company already made me an offer?+

Yes — urgently. Insurance companies make fast, low offers because unrepresented claimants accept them. ZEUS consistently recovers 3x to 10x the initial offer.

Can I afford a personal injury attorney?+

Yes. ZEUS works on contingency — you pay nothing upfront. Our fee comes from the settlement we recover for you. If we don't win, you owe nothing.
