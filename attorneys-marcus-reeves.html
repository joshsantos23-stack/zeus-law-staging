About Marcus Reeves

Marcus Reeves founded ZEUS Law Firm after spending the first decade of his career as insurance defense counsel. He watched firsthand how carriers minimize payouts to injured victims — and decided to switch sides.

Since founding ZEUS, Marcus has personally recovered over $30 million for Los Angeles injury victims, including multiple verdicts and settlements exceeding $1 million. He has tried over 40 cases to verdict in Los Angeles County Superior Court.

His philosophy: prepare every case for trial. Insurance companies settle cases higher when they know trial is real. Marcus never bluffs — and carriers know it.
