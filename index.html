COMMON QUESTIONS

Answers From Our Attorneys.

How much does a personal injury lawyer in Los Angeles cost?+

At ZEUS Law Firm, we work on a contingency fee basis — you pay nothing unless we win. No upfront costs, no retainer, no hourly fees.

How long do I have to file a personal injury claim in California?+

California's statute of limitations for most personal injury cases is 2 years. Government entity cases may have a 6-month deadline.

What is my personal injury case worth?+

Case value depends on medical expenses, lost wages, future care costs, and pain and suffering. We've recovered settlements from $100K to over $5 million.

How long will my case take?+

Most cases settle within 6 to 18 months. Complex cases or those going to trial can take longer.

Do I need a lawyer if the insurance company already made me an offer?+

Yes — and urgently. ZEUS consistently recovers 3x to 10x the initial insurance offer. We'll review any offer for free.
