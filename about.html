Our Story

ZEUS Law Firm was founded on a simple premise: injured people deserve representation that fights as hard as they do. Too many firms are settlement factories — they churn cases, settle fast, and move on. ZEUS was built as the alternative.

Our founding partners — Marcus Reeves and Sofia Vargas — both came from the insurance defense side. They saw firsthand how carriers exploit unrepresented victims. They switched sides to fix it.

Today, ZEUS has recovered over $50 million for Los Angeles injury victims, with a 99% success rate and 4.9 stars across 312 Google reviews.
